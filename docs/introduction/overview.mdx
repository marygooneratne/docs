Loopscale is a modular lending protocol that enables the overcollateralized lending and borrowing of crypto assets on Solana.

Loopscale uniquely solves the limitations of onchain credit today while also unlocking entirely new products and markets. The protocol combines a robust order book with pool-like abstractions to create a borrowing and lending experience far beyond what legacy DeFi protocols can support.

## Learn about Loopscale

- **Vaults**: Access passive lending exposure by depositing to Loopscale Vaults, each curated with a unique risk profile and strategy.
- **Yield Loops**: One-click leverage for yield-bearing tokens, borrowing at fixed rates to multiply returns from assets like liquidity positions and LSTs.
- **Borrowing**: Borrow at lower, fixed-rates and better LTVs against a diverse range of collateral, including liquidity positions and native staked SOL.
- **Advanced Lending**: Lend capital with precise control over, rates, durations, and eligible collateral based on individual risk appetite and market outlook.

## How does Loopscale compare to typical lending protocols?

Most DeFi lending today relies on pooled liquidity with algorithmic interest rates, a model designed when builders were limited by Ethereum's constraints. As crypto markets mature, the structural flaws of this model are becoming more evident.

Instead of relying on pools, the foundation of the Loopscale protocol is a powerful order book matching borrowers and lenders directly across parameters like rate, collateral type, LTV, payment schedule, and default conditions. This enables:

- **Better rates**: Direct matching eliminates the liquidity requirements present in pool models, closing the rate spread to improve lending yields and reduce borrowing costs.
- **Markets for the next generation of crypto assets**: Flexible market infrastructure supports sophisticated assets like liquidity positions, staked tokens, and cross-protocol positions.
- **Precise risk management**: Isolated collateral, flexible per-asset parameters eliminate systemic risk and one-size-fits-all exposure.

Historically, order book-based lending protocols have struggled with fragmentation and complexity. Loopscale solves this with pool-like abstractions that enable a familiar and user-friendly while preserving the scalability and flexibility of the core protocol.

### The advantages of using Loopscale over traditional lending protocols

- Best Rates: Get the lowest borrowing rate and highest lending yields, made possible by the capital efficiency of order books.
- More Collateral: Use new collateral assets including native-staked SOL, NFT-based LP positions, memecoins, and real-world assets.
- Custom Risk Profiles: Have custom risk exposure by controlling the assets you lend against.
- Fixed Rates: Volatile rates can make strategies unprofitable. Fixed rates enable predictable returns for lenders and borrowers.
