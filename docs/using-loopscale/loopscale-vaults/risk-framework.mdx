**Genesis Vaults** are the first generation of lending Vaults on Loopscale, curated and managed by the Loopscale core team. These Vaults are designed to test and refine Loopscale’s fixed-rate lending infrastructure, and are governed by a robust risk management framework developed specifically for early-phase growth and protocol reliability.

This document outlines how Loopscale approaches vault curation, collateral selection, rate management, and liquidity safety for Genesis Vaults across the Beta, Genesis, and Public rollout phases.

---

## Vault Rollout Strategy

Loopscale’s Genesis Vaults will be deployed in three distinct phases, each designed to progressively increase capital capacity, validate system performance, and onboard a broader user base.

## Collateral Management

### Inclusion Criteria

Collateral for Genesis Vaults is restricted to high-quality Solana-native assets that meet strict safety thresholds:

- **Tokens must have:**

  - Pyth Oracle support
  - Listings on Binance, Coinbase, or ByBit
  - Over $1M in _non-token-incentivized_ DEX liquidity

- **Wrapped or staked assets:**
  - Must inherit eligible underlying assets
  - Require smart contract audits from at least **two Tier-One Solana audit firms**

Token inclusion is based on liquidity, team risk, smart contract reliability, and redemption logic.

> _A weekly maintained list of eligible collateral is available on request._

---

### LTV Selection

Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratios are determined based on:

- **Liquidity** – Can the asset be sold quickly and at scale?
- **Volatility** – Likelihood of rapid price changes
- **Oracle Quality** – Frequency and accuracy of pricing updates

#### Collateral Categories:

- **Vanilla Tokens** – Risk from low DEX liquidity
- **LSTs / LRTs** – Risk from price deviation to redemption value
- **LP Tokens** – Smart contract and pool composition risks
- **Paired Assets** – If principal and collateral are aligned, LTV can reach up to 95%

_Assets that fail these checks (e.g., unaudited, low-liquidity tokens) are excluded._

## Rate Management

Genesis Vaults follow a **utilization-based interest rate model** designed to balance competitiveness and liquidity health.

### Base Curve

- **\<25% Utilization:** 50% of lowest borrow rate on Solana
- **25–50%:** Mirrors lowest floating rate
- **>50%:** Midpoint between market borrow and lend rates
- **SOL Vaults:** Pegged to Solana inflation after 50%

---

### Collateral Weighting

To avoid overexposure, each collateral type has a cap on active loans. Above 10%, interest rates rise:

| Asset Group  | Max Target Share | Rate Increase Per % Above Cap |
| ------------ | ---------------- | ----------------------------- |
| USDC / USDT  | 50%              | +2.5%                         |
| SOL + LSTs   | 33%              | +2.5%                         |
| JLP          | 33%              | +2.5%                         |
| Other Assets | 10%              | +2.5%                         |

---

### Duration Premiums

Longer durations will have:

- **+10% of base rate per week**
- **Caps on term exposure** (see Liquidity Management)

## Liquidity Management

Loopscale prioritizes real-time liquidity with automated guardrails.

### Duration Caps

| Term     | Max % of Total Vault Debt |
| -------- | ------------------------- |
| 1 day    | 100%                      |
| 1 week   | 50%                       |
| 1 month  | 20%                       |
| 3 months | 10%                       |

### Liquidity Thresholds

- **5% Idle Buffer:** New originations pause below this
- **1% Emergency Threshold:** Loans are offloaded or paused
- **Withdrawal Queues:** Activated if liquidity isn’t restorable

### Idle Liquidity Strategy

Idle funds may be temporarily deployed to MarginFi for passive yield—removed if risk grows.

---

## Vault Fees

- **$0 Fees** until the Vault exceeds $25M TVL
- **10% performance fee** on interest above that
- If post-fee returns are noncompetitive, fees may be waived

---

## Liquidation Framework

Loopscale uses a **redundant three-tier liquidation engine** for maximum reliability.

### Tiers

1. **Cloud Liquidator** – Websocket-triggered flash loan liquidation
2. **Polling Agent** – 5-minute interval checks from a reserve wallet
3. **Manual Intervention** – On-call team intervention if others fail
